Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

UWB Scanner ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Android application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. UWB Scanner operates entirely on your device and does not transmit any data to external servers.

Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device:

  • UWB Configuration Settings: Your preferred ranging parameters (channel, preamble, session ID, etc.)
  • Session Recordings: If you use the recording feature, ranging measurements are saved locally
  • Tracked Devices: Device names and MAC addresses you choose to save
  • Proximity Alerts: Alert configurations you create

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • UWB Ranging: Required to communicate with UWB devices
  • Location: Required by Android for UWB functionality (we do not track your location)
  • Bluetooth: Optional, used for BLE device discovery
  • Internet: Used only for Google Play Billing (in-app purchases)
  • Foreground Service: For background ranging functionality

Google Play Billing

If you purchase the Pro version, the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play. We do not have access to your payment information. We only receive confirmation that a purchase was made.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing: For in-app purchases. Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Firebase Analytics: We collect anonymized usage data to improve the app. This includes app usage patterns, feature engagement, and device information (device model, OS version). No personal information is collected.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: We collect crash reports to fix bugs and improve stability. Crash reports include device information and app state at the time of the crash. No personal information is collected.

All analytics data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify individual users.

Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage mechanisms. Session recordings and settings are stored in your app's private directory, accessible only to the app.

Children's Privacy

UWB Scanner is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
